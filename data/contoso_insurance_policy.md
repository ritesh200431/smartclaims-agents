# Contoso Insurance — General Policy Document
## Policy Effective Date: January 1, 2024
 
## 1. Coverage Types
 
### 1.1 Auto Insurance Coverage
- Collision Coverage: Damage from accidents with vehicles or objects. Maximum coverage up to actual cash value.
- Comprehensive Coverage: Non-collision events — theft, vandalism, weather damage, animal strikes, falling objects.
- Liability Coverage: Mandatory minimum $50,000/person, $100,000/accident bodily injury, $25,000 property damage.
- Uninsured Motorist: Protection if at-fault driver has no insurance. Up to $100,000/accident.
- Medical Payments: Medical expenses for policyholder and passengers, up to $10,000/person.
 
### 1.2 Property Insurance Coverage
- Dwelling Coverage: Protects physical structure up to insured value.
- Personal Property: Belongings covered up to 70% of dwelling coverage.
- Loss of Use: Additional living expenses if home uninhabitable. Max 24 months or 20% of dwelling coverage.
- Other Structures: Detached garages, fences, sheds up to 10% of dwelling coverage.
 
### 1.3 Health Insurance Coverage
- Inpatient Hospital: 80% after deductible.
- Outpatient Services: 70% after deductible.
- Prescription Drugs: Tier 1 (Generic) $10 copay, Tier 2 (Brand) $35 copay, Tier 3 (Specialty) 30% coinsurance.
- Preventive Care: 100% covered, no deductible, in-network only.
- Mental Health: Covered at parity with medical/surgical benefits.
- Annual Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $8,500 individual / $17,000 family.
 
### 1.4 Life Insurance Coverage
- Term Life: 10, 20, and 30-year terms. $100,000 to $5,000,000.
- Whole Life: Permanent coverage with cash value. Guaranteed death benefit.
- Beneficiary: Primary and contingent beneficiaries may be designated at any time.
 
### 1.5 Liability Insurance Coverage
- General Liability: Third-party bodily injury and property damage up to $1,000,000/occurrence.
- Umbrella Policy: Additional $1,000,000 to $5,000,000 above underlying limits.
- Professional Liability: Add-on for professional services errors and omissions.
 
## 2. Exclusions and Limitations
 
### 2.1 General Exclusions (All Coverage Types)
NOT covered: Intentional acts, war/terrorism, nuclear hazard, government action, wear and tear, pre-existing conditions (12-month waiting period for health).
 
### 2.2 Auto-Specific Exclusions
NOT covered: Racing/speed contests, ride-sharing/delivery services (without commercial rider), commercial use vehicles (without endorsement), custom parts exceeding $5,000 (unless scheduled).
 
### 2.3 Property-Specific Exclusions
NOT covered: Flood damage (separate policy required), earthquake (endorsement available), mold exceeding $10,000, home business equipment exceeding $2,500.
 
## 3. Claims Filing Procedures
 
### 3.1 How to File a Claim
1. Report within 72 hours: Phone 1-800-CONTOSO, online claims.contoso-insurance.com, or mobile app.
2. Provide: Date, time, location, description, involved parties.
3. Document: Photos, police reports, medical records, repair estimates.
4. Assignment: Licensed adjuster assigned within 2 business days.
5. Investigation: Adjuster reviews docs, may do on-site inspection.
6. Resolution: Typically 30-60 days. Complex claims up to 90 days.
 
### 3.2 Required Documentation by Type
- Auto: Police report, damage photos, repair estimates, other driver info.
- Property: Damage photos/video, damaged items inventory, police report.
- Health: Itemized medical bills, EOB, prescription records.
- Life: Certified death certificate, claim form, beneficiary ID proof.
 
## 4. Deductibles
 
### 4.1 Auto Deductibles
Options: $500, $1,000, $1,500. Per incident, not per policy period.
 
### 4.2 Property Deductibles
Options: $1,000, $2,500, $5,000. Hurricane/wind: 2% of dwelling (coastal zones).
 
### 4.3 Health Deductibles
Standard: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family. HDHP: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family. Preventive care exempt.
 
## 5. Renewal and Cancellation
 
- Auto-renew annually. 30-day advance notice of premium changes.
- Policyholder cancellation: Anytime with written notice. Pro-rata refund (up to 10% short-rate penalty).
- Insurer cancellation: Non-payment (30-day grace), misrepresentation, increased risk. 60-day written notice.
 
## 6. Fraud Policy
 
### 6.1 Definition
Intentional deception for unauthorized benefit: false/inflated claims, staged incidents, application misrepresentation, falsified documents.
 
### 6.2 Detection
AI-powered systems evaluate: claim patterns, geographic/temporal anomalies, network analysis, document authenticity.
 
### 6.3 Consequences
Immediate denial, policy cancellation (no refund), law enforcement referral, civil recovery, NICB reporting.
